Project 1: Braiding Machine (Course-Engineering Exploration)

Braiding Machine(Engineering Exploration) a semi-automatic, compact, and lightweight device that is portable, and can braid thread/yarn. The machine was controlled with an app integrated with Arduino. The LCD display indicated the progress.

Project 2: Artificial Reservoir (Course-Design Thinking and Innovation)

Farmers in Unkal village needed a way to address water scarcity (might be due to insufficient rain or depleted ground water level) and access the facilities to reserve available water at affordable cost to achieve more productivity and higher yield. Our project dealt with building an efficient but cost-friendly artificial reservoir to conserve rainwater and contacting a suitable stakeholder to issue loans for construction of such a reservoir.
